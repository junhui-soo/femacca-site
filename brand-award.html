Brand Award

The Malaysia Brand Award - Is mainly focusing on local and international industries, especially well-know brands created by chinese community in the country in order to commend and accord recognition to them.

For a brand to achieve "well-know" status, its products would possess excellent and first-rated qualities compared to others in the same industries. While for consumers perspective, the brand has become credible and favoured one in the public. Hence, based on the success story of these famous branding, it will be encouraging to other entrepreneurs in the same industries to be more creative to build up their brands and also have determination to expand their businesses and contribute to the economic development in the country. Therefore, a success brand will be a representation of a country when it holds well-known status in the people's mind.

The Malaysian Brand Award's logo adopted the square shape which means the winning brands represent righteousness, honesty, integrity, while the brand's culture and value are guaranteed.

The award's portrait is a paper crane. The paper crane is an eastern art and culture. It also means hope and aspiration. This signifies the winner of the Malaysian Brand Award as the hope of the economic development and the industry's development in this country. The success of the brand also gives hopes to the industry and channel positive energy to other related industry.

Through the Malaysian Brand Award which aims to offer recognition to brands, the FEMAGGA would also like to wish the awarded brands all the success in expanding a Malaysian grown business. The association hopes that the brands will take the leap by expanding their businesses into international market after receiving recognition from various industries.

The paper crane in an overlooking position gives the sense of observing from the top, implicating the winner is famous to be able to soar higher among other brands. It also carries the hope that the brands can continue to be amongst the best in the industries.

Dato’ Dr. Pa Nok Ching
(HURIX’S / Jin Bin Group)

Jin Bin Group was incorporated in 1979, led by our distinguished founder Dato’ Dr. Pa Nok Ching, enjoyed sound establishment with setting up Jin Bin Corporation Sdn Bhd and several subsidiaries, J.B. Pharmacy Group Sdn Bhd, Jin Bin (M) Pharmaceutical Group Sdn Bhd, J.B. Laboratories Sdn Bhd, J.B. Inno-Biotech Sdn Bhd and J.B.M.H. Sdn Bhd, actively involved in cultivation, research and development, production, laboratory test and marketing, to ensure only the highest quality products are provided to consumers. The group has successfully making “HURIX’S” into a well-known local and international brand. Being one of the market leader in the production of herbal medicines and healthcare products for more than four decades, Jin Bin had never failed to grow from strength to strength in all phases of the production process, from procurement and testing or raw ingredients, combining outstanding formulations by their qualified modern masters of Traditional Chinese Medicine with exceptionally strict standards, utilizing state-of-the-art equipment and methodologies, packaging to after sales service. Today, Jin Bin Group produces and markets more than 200 types of herbal pharmaceutical products, healthcare supplements, food and beverages local and abroad.
