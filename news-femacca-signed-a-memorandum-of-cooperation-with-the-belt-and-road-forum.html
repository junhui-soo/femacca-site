FEMACCA signed a Memorandum of Cooperation with the Belt and Road Forum

On July 17, the FEMACCA hosted a seminar on "One Belt, One Road" and arranged for a delegation from the China Zhi Gong Party Central Committee to meet with leaders of the Chinese delegation. The seminar was attended by about 100 leaders from various industries. In his speech, Mr. Lu Guo Yi, Deputy Secretary General of the Central Committee Delegation, expressed his hope that Malaysian entrepreneurs would actively participate in the "Belt and Road" project and contribute to the development of the region. Mr. Dai Wei Fa, Organizing Secretary of the General Chamber of Commerce, then gave an overview of the Malaysia-China economic and trade and Belt and Road projects in Malaysia.

In addition, the FEMACCA also signed a memorandum of understanding with the Chinese World Culture and Communication Company Limited to publish a book on the history of the Malaysian Chinese sages in China and Malaysia, so as to bring the achievements of the sages to the world and to perpetuate them in history.
