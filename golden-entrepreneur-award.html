The mission

Inherit the Past and Usher in the Future, Lasting Establishment in Malaysia, Expanding to the World

In 1920, 18 Chinese industry leaders founded the [Hang Tuan] which is now known as The Federation
of Malaysia Chinese Guilds Association (FEMACGA).

The main objective of the association were to organize programs and activities to bring closer
relationship between community leaders from various industries, protecting the interest and
promoting a favorable business environment for the Chinese merchants and businesses. FEMACGA set
up a school that provides education opportunity to the young generation.

1954, the Registration of Societies approved the registration of Selangor Federation of Chinese Guilds
Association and since played an important role in politics, economy, culture and education and culture
issue, cultivate friendly relationship, unity and create better opportunities amongst the members. In
2003, the association expanded its growth and was renamed as [Hang Tuan Zong Hui] while remained
its English name as “The Federation of Malaysia Chinese Guilds Association” (FEMACGA).
The Association held the 1st Malaysia Golden Entrepreneur Award in 2013 after its transformation. The
Awards is to recognize and honor the excelling entrepreneur leader and share their successful stories
as example to encourage and give inspiration in order to motivate other entrepreneur elevating their
business brings the country economy growth together. In 2015 & 2017 had successfully organized 2nd
& 3rd Malaysia Golden Entrepreneur Award.

To be in lined with transformation and internationalization, the association change its Chinese name
and remain its english name as “The Federation of Malaysia Chinese Guilds Association (FEMACGA)”
The association believe through the transformation that build the brand new image and strong
reputation bring the member growth internationally together. The associate had been appointed as
consultant of the Secretariat for the Advancement of Malaysia Entrepreneurs (SAME) under the Prime
Minister’s Department (PMO). Besides that the association had also been appointed on the promotion
and verification on the RM100 million Chinese Small Medium Enterprise Grant which is allocated by
the Federal Government.

Today FEMACGA been expended and consist with 116 affiliate member association. Besides, FEMACGA
have setup a business school to provide wide rage business training, at the same time signed the
strategic partner agreement with TussStar Malaysia to create a platform and opportunities for
entrepreneur business exchange and collaboration with China to help the country economy growth.

Winner

Dato’ Sri Lee Ee Hoe, JP (Apple Vacation Group) 

In 1996, Dato' Sri Lee Ee Hoe and Dato' Sri Koh Yock Heng, two Japanese students from Malaysia established the travel agency known as "Apple Vacations". With an initial investment of RM 15,000, Dato' Sri Lee Ee Hoe, serving as the managing director, was determined to foster the growth of Apple Vacations and transform it into a leading "Malaysian Fun Travel Agency" from the very beginning.
The two founders grew up in the small town of Yong Peng in the state of Johor, Malaysia. They studied together in Japan and, for the past 18 years, have remained dedicated to the original mission of their venture. They have continuously explored new attractions and distinguished themselves in providing immersive themed travel experiences, making Apple Vacations the largest tourism group in Malaysia and a benchmark travel agency for domestic travel brands.
In recent years, Apple Vacations has transformed into the Apple Vacation Group, comprising five major business sectors: tourism, hospitality, media and information services (101 Media), food and beverage, and real estate. The group has expanded its business industry chain and developed into a regional tourism group. It has established branches in Indonesia and Singapore and plans to set up branches in other Southeast Asian countries such as Thailand, Cambodia, and Vietnam.

Future Plans

Currently, the Apple Vacation Group has set its corporate goal as "Apple Vision 2018," aiming to become a renowned employer in the domestic tourism industry with 500 employees and 100 professional tour guides. The group also aims to achieve a revenue of RM 1 billion by 2018 and ensure that its employees receive an annual salary of at least RM 60,000 or more. The ultimate goal of this vision is to take the Apple Group public. Dato' Sri Lee Ee Hoe, the Managing Director of the Apple Group, stated that going public is a commitment to both customers and employees, signifying that Apple has reached a stable stage and is a recognized brand. Going public would be an ideal milestone.
